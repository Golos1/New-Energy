  • Hydroelectric power relies on the energy in water currents to turn a turbine to generate energy, like with wind power.
About 66,500 people are employed in the hydroelectric industry.
Hydroelectric power plants can go from zero to maximum power output very quickly, so they're extremely helpful in emergencies.
Hydroelectric facilities also help with irrigation and flood control.
Pros

Hydropower is reliable, since the rivers and oceans it gets energy from probably aren't changing their location anytime soon.

Cons

Like with wind farms, there are only so many locations where you can build hydroelectric power plants.
